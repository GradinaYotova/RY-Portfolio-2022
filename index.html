My name is Radina Yotova. I am an interactive designer based in between Sofia & The Hague. I've graduated a Bachelor degree in Graphic Design at Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.
I am mainly interested in image-making and storytelling, produced and designed by means of new media technology. Some of the topics I am passionate about are social behavior, participatory culture, network culture and computer vision.
