Switch 2020

visual identity / merchandise / 2020

Switch - Switch 2020 is commissioned visual identity design for the annual charity event ‘Switch’ from Netherlands Dance Theatre. The project was in collaboration with Weronika Uyar.
The sponsors of the performative dancers for the year 2020 was “Plastic Soup Organisation” and the topic was waste in the ocean.
The concept for the visual identity developed around the idea of dancing / motion and plastic waste in the ocean. It's starting point was typographical animations, which later on were translated into logo design, flyer, poster and t-shirt & a tote bag.
