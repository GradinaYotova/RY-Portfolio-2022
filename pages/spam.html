SpamBook

web / type / poster / 2021

In the age of information, there is so little information ... and so much spam…spam…spam…
In order to find some purpose of all the daily spam emails which I am receiving I’ve decided to re-use their content in different playful ways.
The SpamBook is a short experiment which is trying to bring together spam, typography and motion in order to create more dynamic and exciting experience of all this unused data.

Website accessed on: https://gradinayotova.github.io/SPAM-BOOK/
SOME OF THE CONTENT MIGHT NOT BE SUITABLE FOR EPILEPTICS!!!
