The Thinking Machine

poster / web / generated image / 2020

The Thinking Machine is a project about the perception of colour. How colour is perceived by the machines, and all the similarities it has to human brain and memories. It was an exploration of the production of images, and the unique process which it takes.
The final outcome is an interactive poster, which could be seen with an AR app. The second part of the project was about finding a way to exhibit the final result, if there were no limitations at all.
I recreate more spacial and poetic picture of the processes which take place in the machine.
